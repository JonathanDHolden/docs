# Image styles

Current list of responsive image styles:

## Responsive image styles

* 3:2 Image
  * Large 3:2 (768x512)
  * Large 3:2 2x (1536x1024)
* Banner (28:9)
  * Small 28:9 (768x247)
  * Small 28:9 2x
  * Medium 28:9 (1024x329)
  * Large 28:9 (1440x463)
* Hero or Banner
  * Small 21:9 (768x330)
  * Large 21:9 (1440x620)
  * Large 21:9 2x (2880x1240)
* LocalGov Newsroom featured
  * LocalGov 248x181
  * LocalGov 535x302
* Square
  * Square Large
  * Square Medium
